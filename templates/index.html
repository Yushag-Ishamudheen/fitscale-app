Welcome to FitScale

FitScale is a lightweight fitness helper: calculate your BMI, learn which category you belong to, and get tailored meal plans, workouts, and tips from FitBot — a guided, option-driven assistant (no external AI required).

Why BMI?

BMI is a simple metric to estimate body composition. Use it as a quick health guideline — FitBot will give tailored tips based on your BMI category.

How FitBot works

  1. Open FitBot tab
  2. Select your BMI category (or calculate it with the BMI tab)
  3. Choose a topic: Meal Plans, Workouts, or Key Suggestions
  4. Thank FitBot to end the session

Touch & Mobile Friendly

Large buttons and touch support make this app work smoothly on phones and tablets.
